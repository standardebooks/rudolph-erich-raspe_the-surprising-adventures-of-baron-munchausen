The Surprising Adventures of Baron Munchausen
was published in 1785 by
Rudolph Erich Raspe.

This ebook was produced for the
Standard Ebooks project
by
Alex Cabal,
and is based on a transcription produced in 2006 by
Emma Dudding, Dagny, John Bickers, and David Widger
for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans available at the
Internet Archive.

The cover page is adapted from
Baron Münchhausen Underwater,
a painting completed before 1896 by
Gottfried Franz.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
